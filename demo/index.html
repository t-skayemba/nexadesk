Customer Support That Answers Itself

NexaDesk is an embeddable AI support widget that answers questions from your knowledge base, knows when to hand off to a human agent, and logs every conversation. Drop one script tag onto any website and it's live.

What's Included

Everything in the box

Built as a complete, deployable system — backend, widget, logging, and escalation all wired together out of the box.

🧠

Knowledge Base Answers

The bot searches your custom knowledge base before every reply, so answers are always grounded in your content — not hallucinated. Easy to update as your product evolves.

🔁

Intelligent Escalation

Detects frustration, billing disputes, security concerns, and direct requests for a human. Hands off cleanly with full conversation context so your agent is never starting from scratch.

📋

Conversation Logging

Every message is stored with timestamps and escalation flags. Review what customers ask most, spot gaps in your knowledge base, and audit any conversation.

🎨

Full White-Label

Your colours, your logo, your bot name — configurable without touching code. The widget adapts to any brand and fits visually on any website.

⚡

One-Line Deployment

One script tag is all a client adds to their site. Works on any website — WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, plain HTML. No framework or build step required.

📊

Admin Log Endpoint

A built-in API endpoint exposes all conversation summaries for review. Extendable into a full dashboard — filter by date, escalation status, or topic.
